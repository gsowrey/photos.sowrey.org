@# England

A bit of a catch-all, this album is for things that aren't in [London](/london+uk/), [York](/york/), or [Liverpool](/liverpool/) _proper_. So if Google Maps says it isn't in one of those, it's in here. At least until I've been to more places in the UK, in which case I'll break them out more.
